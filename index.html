Mid-South's experimental wireless data network

HamWAN Memphis Metro is a non-profit building and operating a wide area WiFi network for use by tinkerers, hobbyists, and emergency preparedness groups.

  • Independent

    Use our network to pass traffic across town, even when the internet is down.

  • Free

    Community driven and supported, connecting to our network is free and there is no registration process.

  • Fast

    With a clear view of one of our cell sites, speeds up to 40 Mbps are possible.

  • Easy to Join

    Operating in FCC Part 97 RF spectrum, all licensed amateur radio operators are welcome to participate.
